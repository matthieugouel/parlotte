*Last updated: 2026-04-19*

Parlotte is a Matrix messaging client. The developers of Parlotte do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

## Data Parlotte handles on your device

Parlotte stores the following locally, on your Mac, and nowhere else:

- **Matrix credentials** (access token and user identifier) — stored in the macOS Keychain.
- **Encryption keys** (device keys, room keys, recovery backup) — stored in an on-device SQLite database managed by `matrix-rust-sdk`.
- **Message history and timeline state** — cached locally for offline access.
- **App preferences** (notification toggles, profile name, appearance) — stored in `UserDefaults`.

Uninstalling Parlotte removes all of the above.

## Data Parlotte sends over the network

Parlotte connects only to the Matrix homeserver you configure. All Matrix protocol traffic — messages, presence, device verification, media uploads — goes directly to that homeserver.

- We do not operate a homeserver.
- We do not proxy, inspect, or log any of your traffic.
- The homeserver's privacy policy applies to data it receives from you. Choose a homeserver you trust, or run your own.

## Third parties

- **Your chosen Matrix homeserver** receives your Matrix traffic.
- **Apple** may collect diagnostics and crash reports if you opt into sharing them with developers (standard macOS behavior; see Apple's privacy policy).
- No analytics SDKs. No advertising SDKs. No fingerprinting. No trackers of any kind.

## Notifications

Parlotte uses **local notifications only**. They are generated by your Mac when the app receives a new message from your homeserver. No notification content is sent to Apple's push servers or any third party.

## Encryption

Private rooms in Parlotte are end-to-end encrypted using the Matrix Megolm protocol. Your encryption keys are generated and stored on your device. The developers of Parlotte cannot read your encrypted messages.

## Account deletion

- **Remove Parlotte**: drag the app to the trash. All local data goes with it.
- **Delete your Matrix account**: use your homeserver's account deactivation flow. Parlotte does not operate account infrastructure.

## Contact
