DESCRIPTION (max 4000 chars)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Parlotte is a fast, privacy-first Matrix messaging client, built natively for macOS.

The Matrix network is open, federated, and end-to-end encrypted. You choose which server to trust, and your conversations stay between you and the people you're talking to. Parlotte makes that experience feel as polished as the proprietary messaging apps you're used to — without the tracking, lock-in, or compromises.

WHY PARLOTTE
• Truly native. SwiftUI from the ground up, no Electron, no wrapped web view.
• Fast. Persistent sync keeps your messages live without polling.
• Private by default. End-to-end encryption for every private room, with cross-signing and device verification.
• Resilient. Key backup and recovery mean reinstalling never costs you your encrypted history.
• Focused. Local notifications that respect your attention — banners only for rooms you're not looking at.
• Flexible. Multiple profiles for separating personal and work accounts.
• Polished. Light and dark themes that match your system appearance.

FEATURES
• Rich text messages with reactions, replies, edits, deletes, and typing indicators.
• Image, file, video, audio, and location messages.
• Public room directory and invite handling.
• Room settings: name, topic, encryption indicator, member list.
• Self-verification via SAS emoji comparison.
• Recovery-key backup and device cross-signing.
• Unread indicators and read receipts.
• Infinite message history pagination.

PRIVACY
Parlotte collects nothing. Your messages and credentials stay on your device and on the homeserver you pick. No telemetry. No trackers. No ads.

YOU NEED A MATRIX ACCOUNT
Sign up on matrix.org, or use any public or self-hosted homeserver running Synapse or a compatible Matrix server. Parlotte works with any standard Matrix homeserver.

REQUIREMENTS
macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later.
